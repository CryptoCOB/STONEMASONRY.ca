# Simcoe Stone Masonry - SEO Optimization Summary

## Overview

The Simcoe Stone Masonry website has been comprehensively optimized for search engines with a focus on maximizing visibility in the Ontario masonry market. This document summarizes the key SEO improvements implemented.

## Key SEO Enhancements

### 1. Technical Foundation
- ✅ Enhanced meta tags across all pages
- ✅ Implemented React Helmet for dynamic SEO management
- ✅ Created comprehensive XML sitemap
- ✅ Optimized robots.txt with crawler directives
- ✅ Improved page load performance via caching headers

### 2. Structured Data
- ✅ Implemented JSON-LD for Local Business
- ✅ Added service-specific structured data
- ✅ Included geographic service areas
- ✅ Added business hours and availability
- ✅ Enhanced with social profiles and contact methods

### 3. Page-Specific Optimization
- ✅ Home page: Primary keywords and value proposition
- ✅ Portfolio: Project-specific optimization with rich media
- ✅ About: Company information and trust signals
- ✅ Contact: Location and availability information
- ✅ Emergency Services: Urgency signals and rapid response indicators

### 4. Mobile & PWA Optimization
- ✅ Enhanced manifest.json for Progressive Web App support
- ✅ Added mobile-specific display properties
- ✅ Optimized viewport settings for all devices
- ✅ Implemented app shortcuts for quick access

### 5. Content Enhancement
- ✅ Keyword-rich page titles and descriptions
- ✅ Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy
- ✅ Enhanced Open Graph tags for social sharing
- ✅ Location-specific content for improved local search
